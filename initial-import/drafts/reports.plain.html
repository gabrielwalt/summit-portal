Recommendation

Run the flagship program as 1 portfolio, not 4 projects

Capital and governance choices that keep experience, SEO, and speed moving together.

recommendationlist

Fund the four templates under one program | Align Women's, homepage, Jordan, and Nike By You under shared component, tag, and media rules so each fix compounds instead of competing for the same engineers. | priority

Use one executive scorecard each quarter | Review lab experience, SEO health, organic trend, and AI citation mix in the same forum so tradeoffs stay visible and no single metric hides risk. | action

Recommendation

Plan for weaker organic before branded efficiency stops masking it

Demand resilience when concentration is this high. Branded share above nine in ten is efficient until volume slips. Make the downside visible early.

recommendationlist

Set a public non-branded growth bar | Name a target band for non-branded organic contribution with marketing and finance so the team invests before concentration shows up as a revenue surprise. | risk

Stress-test revenue to continued organic decline | Model what happens if the year-over-year slope on the organic line continues so plans do not assume last year's visit levels. | growth
